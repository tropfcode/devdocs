matplotlib.pyplot.figtext¶

matplotlib.pyplot.figtext(*args, **kwargs)¶

Add text to figure.

Call signature:

text(x, y, s, fontdict=None, **kwargs)

Add text to figure at location x, y (relative 0-1 coords). See text() for the meaning of the other arguments.
